Home Alone

Sunday 9.00 PM on HBO

Home Alone (stylized as HOME ALONe) is a 1990 American comedy film directed by Chris Columbus and written by John Hughes. It is the first film in the Home Alone franchise, and stars Macaulay Culkin, Joe Pesci, Daniel Stern, John Heard, and Catherine O'Hara. The film follows a young boy as he defends his home from two burglars after his family accidentally leaves him behind in Chicago on their vacation to Paris.
